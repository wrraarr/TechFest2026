Zombie Outbreak

Each node starts as a normal survivor. Click one person in the world to infect them and begin the outbreak. Infected players still control their own zombie body, and one extra zombie bot wanders the field slowly and badly. Survivors can move, rotate their bubble shooter, fire at zombies, and duck into houses that grant 10 seconds of safety.

Protocol Rules `g=5/6/3/4/1` moves or stops, `a=1` rotates clockwise, `a=2` rotates counterclockwise, and `a=3` shoots. Infected player nodes are still controlled by their own micro:bit. JSON packets with matching fields work too.
