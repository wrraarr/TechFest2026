Pick up a micro:bit. Play the orchestra.

Every person in the room is part of one string orchestra. Some of you are violins, violas, cellos, or a bass — just press your button to play your note. Someone with the conductor's baton waves it in the air to change how fast and loud everyone plays. Work together and the whole room becomes the music.
