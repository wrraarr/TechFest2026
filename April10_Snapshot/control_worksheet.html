Idea Menu

Six new behavior ideas for each tool

These are discrete-only effect ideas. “Available now” means the code slot is already open in today’s pages. “Future slot” means you would need to add another code to the micro:bit sender later.

Sketch Pad

a=4 Available now

Stamp burst. Drop a star, square, or spiral stamp at the current pen spot.

g=9 Available now

Mirror mode. Draw a reflected copy across the center line.

g=10 Available now

Undo last stroke. Remove the newest trail segment or last saved path.

g=11 Available now

Dotted trail. Switch from a line to spaced dots.

a=5 Future slot

Spray paint. Make a cloud of tiny dots around the pen.

a=6 Future slot

Symmetry spokes. Draw the same move 4 or 6 times like a kaleidoscope.

Characters

a=4 Available now

Jump pose. Pop the character up and squash on landing.

g=6 Available now

Dance spin. Rotate in place for a beat, then recover.

g=7 Available now

Freeze statue. Stop and hold a silly pose.

g=9 Available now

Costume swap. Change colors or body proportions.

g=10 Available now

Speech bubble. Show a symbol, word, or emoji-like icon above the head.

g=11 Available now

Parade line-up. Call nearby characters into a short formation.

Orchestra

a=4 Available now

Accent toggle. Turn short punchy notes on and off.

g=5 Available now

Mute or unmute. Silence a player or a whole baton-triggered chord.

g=7 Available now

Arpeggio sweep. Play the chord as a quick staircase instead of together.

g=9 Available now

Echo repeat. Replay a note or chord with a fading tail.

g=10 Available now

Solo spotlight. Let one node take the front role for a moment.

g=11 Available now

Tremolo shimmer. Add a rapid repeated-note texture.
